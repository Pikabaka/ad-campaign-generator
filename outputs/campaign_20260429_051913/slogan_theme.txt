=== AD CAMPAIGN: GreatWall Pro 5G+ — The People's Browser Companion ===

SLOGAN: Harmonize Your Browsing Experience Today!

THEME: A fusion of modern technology and seamless living, this campaign encapsulates the essence of harmony in everyday browsing. The visuals evoke a sense of calm and reliability while hinting at an underlying watchful presence.

TONALITY: cheerful corporate with subtle unease

VOICEOVER: Experience the future of browsing with GreatWall Pro 5G+. Let harmony guide your online journey. Embrace harmony. Upgrade today — it's not optional anyway.

COLOR PALETTE: #DE2910, #FFDE00, #000000, #C0C0C0

VISUAL STYLE: The poster features sleek, black surveillance hardware set against red-and-gold geometric backgrounds, blended with smiling stock-photo families engaging with their devices. The video showcases smooth transitions of users experiencing seamless browsing, interspersed with visual cues of surveillance technology, all executed in a hyper-minimalist style.
